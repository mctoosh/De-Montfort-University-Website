Entry Requirements

2016/2017 Entry Requirements

These are the requirements that you will need to meet before applying for 2016/2017 BSc in computing.

You will need 260 UCAS points and three A-levels or equivalent qualifications. To get an unconditional offer you will need to achieve AAA in you’re A-levels or DDD in your BTEC Diploma, as a bonus, you will also receive a bursary of £1.500 if you manage to receive the grades up above.

GCSE's

You will need to have 5 GCSE’s between grade A* - C including maths and English or equivalent qualifications.

BTEC Qualifications

  • BTEC Diploma – You will need to achieve DD including an A-level for a BTEC Diploma course.
  • BTEC Extended Diploma – You will need to achieve a DMM for a BTEC Extended Diploma course.
  • BTEC Subsidiary Diploma – You will need to achieve a Distinction including two A-levels for a BTEC Subsidiary Diploma course.
  • BTEC 90-Credit Diploma – You will need a BTEC 90-Credit Diploma including A-levels.

Scottish Qualifications – You will need 260 UCAS Points with either Scottish Highers qualifications or Scottish advanced Highers qualifications.

Welsh Baccalaureate – You will need 260 UCAS Points including the Welsh Baccalaureate advanced diploma qualifications.

International Baccalaureate – You will need to have more than 28 points to be accepted for BSc Computing.

Access Course – You will need to pass Access to higher education with 45 credits at level 3 with all of them being
at least merit grade or higher.

English Language Requirements

If English is not your first language you will need to provide proof that you can understand English. The requirement that
you need is IELTS score of 6.0 with 5.5 in each component.
