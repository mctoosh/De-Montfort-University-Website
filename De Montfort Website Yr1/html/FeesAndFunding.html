Fees And Funding

Loans And Grants

As a student, you will receive a full grant of £3,387 as long as your family income is £25.000. You do not have to pay back the full grant as long as your family income is £25.000 or less, however if it is between £25.001 and £42.620 you may be eligible to receive some grant.

You may also receive maintenance loan which will depend on your household income. If you do not live in the city or you live away from your home the maximum amount that you can receive is £5,740, however if you do live in the city, you will receive a maximum amount of £4,565.

As for the tuition loan, you do not have to pay the fee upfront, however, you will need to repay the full amount back to the government which only begins if you receive more than £21.000 a year. For example, if you receive £21.500 annually, you will have to repay £4 every month.
