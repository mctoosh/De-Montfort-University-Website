Careers

Careers

There is a high demand for computing graduates, where all the students that graduated recently were either working or went into the further study. There are a variety of fields that you could choose from whether it be a system analyst, software engineer, systems engineer, computer engineer, database manager, application and web developer, Computing or ICT teacher, and much more. Many of the graduates have gone on to different types of careers companies such as BAE systems, Bloomberg, LogicaCMG, Accenture, Hewlett-Packard, Home Office, Ask 4, Network Rail, Lloyds Banking Group and much more.

After you graduate you will have the skills and knowledge to be able to work in your new career. You will be able to know how to design, specify and develop software systems, you will know how to implement and perform professional project management. All these skills are very highly sought after.
