Teaching And Assessment

In this course, you will have mixture of Course works and exams. You will be given a timetable which includes, Lectures, Computer lab sessions, tutorials, this will usually be around 12-16 hours a week. You will also have 14- 16 hours of independent study a week to complete any assignments and revisions. You also will need to give some time for your group to complete any group works.

You will also have your own personal tutor that will assist you and give you advice concerning academic matters, welfare concerns, and career development. You will be able to visit your personal tutor any time during office hours or you can just book an appointment with your tutor.

For each module you will need to meet many different types of specified learning outcomes, for example, phase tests, exams, reports, portfolios, class presentations, lab exercises and much more.

Facilities

Gateway House

As a computing student, you will have 80 computers available to at Gateway House. There are four divided computer labs for you to work on each of them having at least 20 HP computers for you to work on.

Majority of the software packages are open sources where this means you will be able to download and run the software at your home. There are areas in the computer labs where you will be able to study individually or as a group. You will also be able to find printers to work on nearly every floor in gateway house.

Kimberly Library

The Kimberly Library is open to students 24 hours 7 days a week during the term times. There is also a caf√© located within the library if you need to take a break from your studies. There are 1.500 different study places and 650 computer workstations on the campus. There are over half a million book publications and many electronic journals available for students.

The learning zones could be used for either studying independently, working as a group, or working on a presentation. The library provides you with rooms that include whiteboard, laptop power supplies and DVD players to make you studying much easier.

Accommodation

There are around 2,300 accommodations which all are easy walking distance to the university campus. There are many high quality accommodations rooms to offer our students which include nine halls of residence. You have the choice of choosing weather you want same-gender flat room or mixed flat room, laundry facilities, shared kitchen, internet access or furnished bedrooms.
